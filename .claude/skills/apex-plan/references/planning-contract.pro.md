The operator’s project intent is captured or missing intent is flagged.2. The work is decomposed into at least one epic or one task group unless the request is too ambiguous.3. Every task draft has a status, expected output, acceptance criteria, priority proposal, due-date proposal, and dependency field.4. Dependency, priority, and focus recommendations are labeled as proposals.5. The operator gate is visible.6. Exact computation and mutation are delegated to the correct downstream package.
