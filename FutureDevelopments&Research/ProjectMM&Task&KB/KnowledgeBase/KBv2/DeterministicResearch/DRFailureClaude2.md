> **"Output B was structured as a template, not a deliverable."**

My prompt fixed this with enforcement (XML tags, "write X not describe X" rules, meso structure specs). The working prompt fixes it differently — by making the _deliverable itself_ the natural output of the research mission. When you brief a senior engineer with "produce a meso implementation map with these fields for these 10 steps," the completion drive fills it. When you brief them with "fill this template and emit CONTAMINATIONGUARD if you don't," they fill the template header and stop.
