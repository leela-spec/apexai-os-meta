## Your Setup: 5-Seat Team Plan

You plan: 2 seats (you) + 2 seats (partner) + 1 seat (the society/automation entity). This is exactly the minimum for the **Claude Team Plan**, which requires 5 seats.

**Current pricing (from claude.com, June 2026):**

- Standard seat: **$20/seat/month** (annual) or **$25/seat/month** (monthly)[](https://claude.com/pricing)
    
- Premium seat: **$100/seat/month** (annual) or **$125/seat/month** (monthly)[](https://claude.com/pricing)
    
- 5 standard seats = **$100/month (annual)** or **$125/month (monthly)**
    

---

## Q1: Can Two Seats Merge Into One?

**No.** Seats are strictly individual accounts — there is no seat-merging feature. Each seat = one login, one usage allocation, one independent context. Two seats cannot pool into a single super-account. You can share **Projects** (shared documents/instructions) across seats within one org, but usage quotas remain per-seat.[](https://claude.com/pricing)

---

## Q2: Data Privacy — Training Protection

**Team Plan = contractual guarantee: no model training on your data by default.** This is NOT opt-out like Pro/Max — it's the default, baked into commercial terms. The official claude.com pricing page explicitly states: _"No model training on your content by default"_.

|Plan|Training on your data|Protection type|
|---|---|---|
|Free / Pro / Max|Opt-out toggle|Consumer terms, no DPA|
|**Team (Standard or Premium)**|**Never by default**|**Commercial terms, contractual**|
|Enterprise|Never|+ audit logs, custom retention, HIPAA|

**Critical point:** Pro ($17–$20/mo) does NOT offer this guarantee contractually — it's only an opt-out toggle. The Team plan is the minimum tier where business-grade data protection starts.

---

## Q3: Can Tokens Be Shared Between Seats?

**No.** Token/usage quotas are strictly per-seat and do not pool. If Seat A hits its limit, it cannot borrow from Seat B or Seat C. Each seat resets on its own 5-hour window cycle. The only workaround: assign **Premium seats** ($100/mo) to heavy users, Standard seats ($20/mo) to lighter users — the Team plan allows mixing seat types.

---

## Q4: Isolation Between Seats (Society Seat vs. Personal Seats)

Each seat = a separate account with its own login. Data isolation is **by design** — seats do not share conversation history, Projects, or memory unless you explicitly share a Project with other members. Your society seat can be kept entirely siloed:[](https://claude.com/pricing)

- Give it its own email/login
    
- Do NOT add it to any shared Projects your personal seats use
    
- Admin controls let you manage what connectors/integrations each seat uses[](https://claude.com/pricing)
    

The admin console (central billing & administration, SSO support) gives the Team owner visibility and control over all 5 seats.[](https://claude.com/pricing)

## Key Flags for Your Use Case

- **Society seat isolation:** Fully feasible — keep it on a separate login, separate Projects. No cross-contamination by default.
    
- **Token sharing:** Not possible — plan per-seat load accordingly; use Premium seat if one user (e.g., the automation society account) will be high-volume.
    
- **Data protection:** Team plan is the minimum required threshold for business use with confidential event/billing/client data.
    
- **Seats cannot be merged:** Two seats = two independent users, always.
