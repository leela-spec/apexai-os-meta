## Candidate placement rules

- **ESSENCE.md:** only compression, boundary, and activation rules.
- **BEST_PRACTICES.md:** reusable operating rules with appendix pointers.
- **MISTAKES.md:** failure patterns that the agent must recognize and counteract.
- **TEMPLATES.md:** schemas, tables, and reusable file/audit/terminology shapes.
- **LEARNING_QUEUE.md:** unresolved or promotion-dependent candidates only.
- **Appendices:** detailed source comparisons, ledgers, and evidence.

## Candidate status notes

- **strong_candidate:** high source support and direct fit for KB-base use.
- **candidate:** useful but narrower, adjacent, or requiring more validation.
- **provisional-boundary:** should be visible but not normalized into hard law.
