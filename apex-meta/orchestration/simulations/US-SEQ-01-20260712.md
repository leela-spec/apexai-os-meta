## Verdict: PASS (partial) — honest reading

The story's completion condition ("Alex approves a method and offer whose ... pilot evidence plan ...
have passed independent validity and macro-alignment review") is a long arc that requires a real
operator strategic decision AND a real human-run pilot (stage 9). Neither can be simulated without
fabrication. What the **system itself** owns through stage 2 ran for real and behaved per contract.

Strongest evidence the system works: **the review loop caught 3 genuine defects the strategy author
missed** — (1) Thesis B asserted an "existing clientele / delivery channel / scheduled workshops" that
the sources do not support and that contradicted the memo's own uncertainty U3; (2) the recommendation
overstated A as "foreclosing nothing" when A's specific positioning content is itself a committing
choice; (3) two line anchors were wrong (a pass/fail_condition swap, a phase-arc mis-cite). The
deterministic aggregation forced a bounded, diff-proven v2 that then passed a scoped re-review against
the real sources. A rubber-stamp reviewer would have carried an unsourced market claim into the
operator's decision.
