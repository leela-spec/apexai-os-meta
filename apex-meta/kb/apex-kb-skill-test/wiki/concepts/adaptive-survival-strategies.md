## Definition
In the NARM source, adaptive survival structures arise when core needs are chronically unmet in early life. The source links them to impaired core capacities and names five structures corresponding to [[five-core-needs]]: contact, attunement, trust, autonomy, and love/sexuality survival strategies. Source pointers: `ET-Heller-NARM.md:204-231`, `ET-Heller-NARM.md:509-517`.
