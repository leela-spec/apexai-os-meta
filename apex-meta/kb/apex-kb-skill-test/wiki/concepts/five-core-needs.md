## Definition
In the NARM source, the five core needs are contact, attunement, trust, autonomy, and love/sexuality. The source ties these needs to physical and emotional wellbeing and says their fulfillment supports adult capacities for contact, need-recognition, trust, boundary-setting, and integrated love/sexuality.
