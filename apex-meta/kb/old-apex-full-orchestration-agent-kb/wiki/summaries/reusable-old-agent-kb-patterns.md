# Reusable Old Agent KB Patterns

## Core Summary

The most reusable old-system asset is not the exact old role roster. The durable pattern is the agent doctrine scaffold: compact essence, accepted practices, accepted mistakes, reusable templates, and candidate-only learning. This pattern preserves role behavior, known failure modes, and reusable handoff shapes across sessions without treating unvalidated candidate material as runtime truth.

The anti-sprawl pattern is also reusable: strong specialized behaviors can remain internal modes or checklists inside one role or workflow rather than becoming permanent agents. The scaffold/appendix split is reusable for Apex KB wiki design because compact pages can orient retrieval while deeper pages or appendices preserve evidence, ledgers, examples, and QA traces.
