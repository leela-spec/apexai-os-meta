# Ticketing provider reconciliation

## What this is

Reconciliation between ticketing platform orders, tax settings, payout reports, fees, refunds, and bookkeeping records.

## Practical implication for Lika

Use as the operating bridge from platform exports to accounting entries. Keep gross revenue, platform fee, VAT configuration, refunds, and payout timing separate.
