# LIKA Equinox Findings

- Event case: Equinox / Fundraiser Hamburg.
- Official venue capacity: 300-350; recommendation: 310-320.
- Minimum bar revenue: EUR 7,500.
- Venue handles bar, admission, security, and cleaning.
- Organizer handles greeting, DJs, performers, crew, and program.
- Strong KB areas: invoices, E-Rechnung, GoBD, KSK, ticketing exports.
- Weak KB areas: venue settlement, 7% vs 19% VAT, support/donation wording.
