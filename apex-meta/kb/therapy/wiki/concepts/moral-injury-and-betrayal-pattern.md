# Moral Injury and Betrayal Pattern

## Claim Label

source_backed_summary

## Summary

The sampled personal working-formulation sources repeatedly describe a pattern around betrayal, exclusion, non-reciprocity, agreement violation, narrative distortion, and a strong drive to correct the social field.

This page preserves that pattern as a source-backed formulation. It is not a diagnosis and should not be treated as a fixed identity claim.
