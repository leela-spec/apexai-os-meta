# Anger as Protector of Grief

## Claim Label

source_backed_summary

## Summary

The sampled source material identifies anger, frustration, and irritation as surface states that may protect grief, hurt, vulnerability, and blocked self-compassion.

The operational value of this page is not to suppress anger. It is to use anger as a doorway into the protected affect underneath it.
