# Adaptive Survival Strategies

## Claim Label

source_backed_summary

## Summary

The NARM theory source frames survival strategies as adaptations that arise when core developmental needs are not sufficiently supported. These strategies can distort present experience, fragment self-experience, and impair contact with self and others.

## Practical Meaning for This KB

This page is the theory-side anchor for later pattern mapping. It should not be used to label a person directly. It should be used to ask whether a current pattern may be functioning as an old adaptation.
