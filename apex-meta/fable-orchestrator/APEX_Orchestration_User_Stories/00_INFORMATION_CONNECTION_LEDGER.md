## Connection conclusions

1. **One operating model:** historical agents contribute role and failure-boundary knowledge; current skills supply reusable procedures and deterministic support; Meta Ops owns their integration.
2. **Two independent milestone questions:** Meta Detective asks whether the output is valid; Meta Strategy asks whether a valid output still serves the macro objective.
3. **One correction principle:** a failed check returns to the owner of the defective claim, requirement, method, artifact, or implementation—not to a generic improvement stage.
4. **One continuity principle:** accepted targets, artifacts, verdicts, state deltas, and next actions live in durable files; chat history is not the system of record.
5. **One restraint principle:** specialists, subagents, skills, deterministic tools, and full milestone loops are activated only when their contribution is material.
